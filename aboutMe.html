Thank you for stopping by - I'm Isabel (she/her)!


I am a fourth-year undergraduate student majoring in Informatics with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction and minoring in Digital Arts at the University of California, Irvine (UCI).


I strive to create systems that will help communities grow by making them as accessible and inclusive as possible. Although there is much for me to learn, I have taken opportunities to improve and apply my design knowledge by being involved as a UX/UI Research Assistant at DapLab and a UX/UI Designer at Commit the Change. I look forward to future opportunities to help broaden my horizon on my design journey.
